ATR 72-500 Teardown ( 2023 )

EAS successfully completed the teardown of 3 ex-Kingfisher ATR 72-500 aircraft MSN 718, 737 & 738 in Chennai, India.

Dornier 228 Teardown ( 2022 )

EAS completed the dismantling of 2 units of Dornier 228 ex North East Shuttles. The teardown took place in Calcutta. The parts have been shelfed and made available to install by operators.

Boeing 757 Teardown ( 2019 )

Dismantled 1 unit of Boeing 757, ex – BB Airways, in Kathmandu. This aircraft was dismantled on behalf of our customer in Atlanta, USA.

Jetstream 41 Teardown ( 2018 )

Ex-Agni Air Jetstream 41 teardown were carried out at TIA, Kathmandu. 3 units of these aircraft were dismantled and parts were shelfed to support our operator clients.
